eldwln

I started learning to be a DJ in 2014 with the electro-house genre. I was inspired by Skrillex to become a DJ because of the way he DJing with his Dubstep. In early 2015, I joined DJ Management and fully played the Trap Dubstep and Twerk genres then stopped in 2019. In the same year, I started learning to produce songs with Fl Studio software on my own. From 2019 - 2022, I became a solo DJ focusing on performing arts, private parties, graduations, and weddings. At the end of 2022, I built an event organizer (Afterlife) and now I have 10 people who have joined afterlife and still running now
